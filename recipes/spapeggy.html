Spapeggy And Meatballs

Ho-yeah! It's Spapeggy Time!

We all grew up on Peggy Platters famous Spapeggy and Meatballs so let's take a minute to learn the secrets the queen herself uses

Ingredients

  • Mega Lo Mart spaghetti pasta
  • Mega Lo Mart canned tomatoe sauce
  • Mega Lo Mart frozen meatballs
  • Mega Lo Mart parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook everything according to package instructions
  2. Combine noodles and sauce and heat sauce
  3. Top with meatballs
  4. Add cheese as desired
  5. Enjoy
